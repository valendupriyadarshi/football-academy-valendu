Hydration Guide

  • • Drink 500ml water upon waking up.
  • • 200-300ml every 20 mins during training.
  • • Electrolytes recommended for sessions > 1 hour.
  • • Monitor urine color for hydration status.

Foods to Avoid

  • Deep fried foods (Samosas, Fries)
  • Sugary sodas & energy drinks
  • Processed meats (Sausages, Salami)
  • Excessive white sugar / sweets

Pre-Training Meal

Consume 2-3 hours before. Focus on complex carbs (Oats, Sweet Potato, Brown Rice) for sustained energy.

Post-Training Meal

Consume within 45 mins. essential for recovery. Protein + Simple Carbs (Whey shake/Chocolate milk + Banana).

Supplements (Consult Doctor)

Whey Protein (Recovery), Creatine (Explosive Power), Multivitamins (Immunity). *Whole food is always better.
