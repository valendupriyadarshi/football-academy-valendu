FAQ & Help

What age groups does Valendu Academy train?
We currently offer professional training for three main batches: U-10 (Foundation), U-15 (Development), and our Elite Senior squad. We accept players starting from age 5 up to 21 years.
How do I register for a trial?
You can register for upcoming trials through our website's Registration Page. Simply fill out the form, and our team will contact you with the trial date and venue details.
What equipment do I need to bring?
For trials and initial training sessions, players must bring their own football boots (studs), shin guards, and water bottles. The academy provides training jerseys, bibs, and balls.
Are the coaches certified?
Yes, all our head coaches hold AIFF or AFC licenses and have prior experience playing or coaching at the professional level. We prioritize quality guidance for every player.
Is there a scholarship program?
We offer merit-based scholarships for exceptional talents who perform well in our district and state-level teams. Financial aid is also available for underprivileged players with high potential.
How often are matches played?
Competitive match practice is held every weekend. Additionally, our elite teams participate in district leagues, inter-academy tournaments, and friendly matches regularly.
What if a player gets injured during training?
Player safety is our priority. We have a certified physio on-site during sessions for immediate care, and we have tie-ups with local hospitals for any serious injuries. All registered players are also covered under basic sports insurance.
