Professional Training Programs

U-10 Foundation Batch

Warm-up Routine

  • Light jogging (5 mins)
  • Dynamic stretching (Arm circles, leg swings)
  • Reaction games for agility
  • Ball familiarity drills (Toe taps)

Technical Drills

  • Basic passing technique (Inside foot)
  • Dribbling through cones (Both feet)
  • Ball control & trapping
  • Introduction to shooting mechanics

Tactical Training

  • Understanding positions (Defender, Striker)
  • Basic spacing & movement
  • Teamwork & communication games

Weekly Improvement Goal

"Master ball control with both feet and enjoy the game."

U-15 Development Batch

Warm-up Routine

  • Jogging & Sprints (10 mins)
  • Dynamic mobility (High knees, butt kicks)
  • Rondo (5v2 possession game)
  • Core activation exercises

Technical Drills

  • Advanced passing patterns (One-touch)
  • 1v1 Attacking & Defending
  • Crossing & finishing drills
  • Long ball accuracy

Fitness & Conditioning

  • Speed & agility ladders
  • Bodyweight strength (Pushups, Squats)
  • Endurance running (Intervals)

Tactical Training

  • Formation discipline (4-3-3, 4-4-2)
  • Pressing triggers & defensive shape
  • Counter-attack strategies

Weekly Improvement Goal

"Enhance tactical awareness and physical endurance."
